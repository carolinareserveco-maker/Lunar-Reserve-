About

Carolina Reserve Botanicals creates small-batch herbal blends made for people who want a smoother, tobacco-free smoking experience. Our blends focus on calm flavor, relaxing aroma, and a clean visual style.


Each blend is packed with selected botanicals like blue lotus, damiana, chamomile, wild dagga, and marshmallow leaf. Because apparently even plants need branding now, and somehow here we are.

These products are not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. Smoking any plant material may irritate the lungs. Must be of legal age to purchase.
