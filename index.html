About

Lunar Reserve by Carolina Reserve was founded around a simple idea: create elegant botanical blends inspired by the calm, reflective atmosphere of the night.


Each blend is crafted from selected botanicals chosen for aroma, flavor, and tradition. Our products are tobacco-free, nicotine-free, and designed for slow evenings, music, conversation, and quiet moments.


The collection includes blends such as Moonveil, Silver Lotus, Nocturne, and Midnight Reverie, each made with its own mood and botanical profile.

These products are not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. Smoking any plant material may irritate the lungs. Must be of legal age to purchase.
